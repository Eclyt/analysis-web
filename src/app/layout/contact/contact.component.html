Analysis refers to the process of breaking down a complex system or concept into smaller, more manageable parts in order to better understand it. It can be applied to many different fields, including mathematics, science, finance, and engineering, among others. In mathematics, for example, analysis is used to study the properties of functions, including continuity, differentiability, and integrability. In finance, analysis is used to evaluate investment opportunities and assess the performance of investment portfolios. In engineering, analysis is used to study the behavior of structures and systems under various conditions.
